pre-heat a frying pan at a low heat
cut eight slices of baguette, each 1 cm thick
beat 1 egg in a bowl
add tablespoon of milk and beat again
add 1/2 tab of butter to the pan
dip slices of baguette in the bowl and then move them to the pan
flip the slices in 3 minutes
cook for another 3
serve with warmed medium amber maple syrup
